Time to Fly! With AstroPods!
Let's take a look at your new home away from home...
Our state of the art and environmentally concious ballons will be lifted up using hydrogen. While being much stronger than helium it is also much more environmentally safe. We will also have solar powered boosters to ensure that the balloon is safe and can be used in case of emergencies.
Taking inspiration from NASA’s Big 60, our balloons will be able to successfully survive the harsh weather conditions of the stratosphere. The balloon is able to hold 60 million cubic feet or 1.7 million cubic meters of gas. With hydrogen’s net lift being 71.05 pounds per 1,000 cubic feet, our balloons will be able to hold roughly 4,263,000 pounds of weight. This will be more than enough to lift the passengers, luggage, and space pod.
The balloons are made out of a polyethylene material which is mostly just plastic. Normally this would be used for different plastic packaging like plastic bags or plastic films. Due to the thin plastic film and the huge size of the balloon, it was able to resist the radical temperatures in the stratosphere.
All space pods come with lounge chairs, a snack bar (specific snacks can be requested beforehand), fridge (specific drinks can be requested beforehand), bluetooth speakers, Wi-Fi, a room for luggage and a bathroom. Transportation for going back to the takeoff zone will also be provided and taken into account when deciding the price of each flight.
